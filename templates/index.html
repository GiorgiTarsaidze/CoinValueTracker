Dogecoin

Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency that originated as a meme, featuring the popular Shiba Inu dog. It was created in 2013 with a lighthearted and fun approach. Despite its origins, Dogecoin has gained significant popularity and a dedicated community. It operates on a decentralized network and utilizes a unique algorithm for mining. Dogecoin has been known for its charitable initiatives and has experienced both price surges and fluctuations in value.

Shiba Inu coin

Shiba Inu coin, also known as SHIB, is a cryptocurrency inspired by the Shiba Inu dog breed. It gained attention in 2021 as a meme token and quickly gained a large following. SHIB operates on the Ethereum blockchain and aims to become the "Dogecoin killer." It features a decentralized ecosystem and offers incentives for holders through staking and token burns. However, it's important to note that SHIB carries high risks and volatility due to its speculative nature and the overall cryptocurrency market.

PEPE

The PEPE memecoin was launched towards the middle of April 2023, with the first entry point on Etherscan being April 18th.Less than three weeks later, the cryptocurrency had a market cap of over $420 million, venturing into the list of the top 100 coins by means of total market capitalization.
